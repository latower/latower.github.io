Welcome to my first Honours Student: [Gianmaria Piergianni](https://gianmariapiergianni.com)!

## The TU Delft Honours Programme
At Delft University of Technology, our top BSc students can apply to the Honours programme. During the 2-year programme, an Honours student will spend 15 ECTS working with a faculty member to do an original piece of research, culminating in a report, a presentation, and ideally also a publication. For more information, see [this website](https://www.tudelft.nl/studenten/ewi-studentenportal/onderwijs/honours-programme).
